Symptoms of Corona Virus

  1. Difficulty in breathing
  2. Cough
  3. Fever
  4. Sore throat
  5. Pneumonia
First 3 are common symptoms but Pneumonia is seen in human with less immunity.Person with better immunity
may not see any symptoms and may not suffer through this disease but he is surely carrier of this virus and
so he may infect any person with less immunity.
If you find these symptoms i.e. cough , fever and specially difficulty in breathing then go and consult your doctor fast .

What Safety Measures to take?

Still antidote and treatment of this virus is not available . We can just take some measures .

  • First and foremost is social distancing . Maintain distance from everybody. So stay home stay safe.
  • Wash your hands with handwash and if possible alchoholic handwash.
  • Cover your face while coughing or sneezing.
  • Avoid touching your nose , eyes and mouth .
  • Eat nutritious food to improve your immunity.
